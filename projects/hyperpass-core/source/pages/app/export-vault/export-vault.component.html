Creating backups of your vault on a regular basis helps ensure you won't lose your important login data. When exporting in the encrypted format, your vault information will be secure. Even if unwanted parties gain access to the file, they will not be able to access your information as long as they do not know your master password.

Format

If you export your vault in the unencrypted or plaintext formats, your vault information will be easily visible to anyone who has access to the file. Unencrypted exports have their uses, but they should not be sent over insecure channels and should be deleted or encrypted as soon as possible.
