Who Is Behind This Project?

Hello! I'm Myles Trevino - the creator of Hyperpass. To my surprise, I found very few options when I was looking for a good password manager. Some had most of the qualities I wanted, but none were quite what I had hoped to find. Because having quick and secure access to all our usernames and passwords is so essential, I believe it's important to have a password manager we truly enjoy using. With Hyperpass, I set out to create a solution that is open-source, available for all platforms, and unified and modern in its functionality and UI. I hope that with this project, more users can find what they're looking for in a password manager.

If you'd like to know more about me and the other projects I've worked on, I encourage you to visit my personal website. Thank you for using my software, and thank you for your support.
