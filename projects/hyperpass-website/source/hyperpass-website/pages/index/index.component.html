Better Digital Security

Unique passwords are an important part of online security. If one of your accounts is compromised, attackers will be unable to access any of your other accounts as long as you're using unique passwords. The password generator included with Hyperpass makes creating good, unique passwords simple, and quick access to autofill lets you be secure without sacrificing practicality.

Peace of Mind

All saved information is encrypted before it is sent to the Hyperpass servers. Your data can only be decrypted using your master password, which never leaves your device. You alone control access to your information. Additionally, following the belief that a policy of transparency is necessary for services entrusted with personal data, the source code of Hyperpass is publicly available.
