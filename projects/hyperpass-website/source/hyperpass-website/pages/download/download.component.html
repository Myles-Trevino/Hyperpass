Available for All Platforms

The Hyperpass browser extensions, mobile apps, and desktop apps are available for download here. You can always access Hyperpass via the Web App, but downloading the dedicated apps for your devices provides a more seamless experience. For example, the browser plugins provide autosave and autofill integration for websites, and the mobile apps offer features such as unlocking with your fingerprint.
